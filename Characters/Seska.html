Seska Imintar

Seska is a young wizard from Korvosa who joined the Acadamae. The party saved her life and she promised to come work for them once she graduated, which she expects to do soon.

Physical Description

Seska is a girl in her late teens, with dark hair and blue eyes. She’s cute but not beautiful.

Personality Profile

Seska is sweet, shy, and brilliant. Kind of Hermione, kind of Emma from Hillside. She's really into magic items and is the best in several years at the Acadamae.

Tactics and Morale

  • Seska is not a combat wizard and prefers to flee when danger threatens.
  • If forced to fight, Seska casts what spells she can. She focuses on buffing allies.
  • Seska surrenders if reduced to fewer than 15 hp.
