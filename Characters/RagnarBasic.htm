Background

Ragnar is one of the Lantern Bearers, the Shin’Rakorath, an elven organization that describes its aim as “to be a light against the coming darkness.” Under the influence of the Winter Council, their goals in practice were the containment and eradication of their people’s most shameful secret, the existence of the drow. After the fall of the Winter Council, however, their focus shifted away from secrecy and genocie and toward open diplomacy and redemption.
